
**Attendees:** Head + Vice Heads
**Goal:** Analyze the previous phase and extract actionable improvements

---

### 1. Opening & Context 

* Purpose of the meeting
* Reminder: focus on **facts, not blame**
* Define the phase being reviewed (Level 1 + Level 2 before exams)

---

### 2. What Went Well

Discuss and list:

* Session quality
* Content clarity
* Student engagement
* Instructor performance
* Team coordination

**Key Question:**
👉 What should we 100% keep doing?

---

### 3. What Didn’t Go Well 

Discuss:

* Weak sessions or topics
* Attendance drop
* Task completion issues
* Timing & workload problems

**Key Question:**
👉 What clearly hurt progress?

---

### 4. Learning Outcomes Reality Check 

Compare **expected vs actual** skills:

* Git
* Programming fundamentals
* Backend concepts
* Problem-solving ability

Identify:

* Major gaps
* Topics that need revision

---

### 5. Engagement & Commitment 

Analyze:

* Attendance trends
* Level 1 → Level 2 transition
* Dropout points

**Key Question:**
👉 When and why did students disengage?

---

### 6. SWOT Snapshot 

Quick brainstorming:

* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Opportunities
* Threats

(No deep discussion here, just listing)

---

### 7. Action Items 

* 3–5 concrete actions
* Assign an owner for each
* Decide what changes next phase

---

### 8. Wrap-up 

* Summary
* Next steps
* Timeline for implementation
